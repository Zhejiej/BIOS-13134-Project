Grow the Best Maize

You're a plant breeder with a CRISPR toolkit, hunting for the best maize, the one with the highest yield, meaning the most kernels a plant can make. Knock out genes that shape the corn plant, its stem cell zone, its height, and its kernel rows, then grow it and see how much corn you get. Results are checked against real maize genetics.
